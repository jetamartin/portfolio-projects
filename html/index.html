About Me

I'm a web developer based in sunny San Diego, California. I develop modern mobile-ready web sites that empower businesses. I pride myself on delivering high quality websites that delight the customer. I'm not happy unless your happy. Here is my process for working with clients:


1

Consultation

We'll discuss your key goals and requirements for your website. I'll create a website proposal that includes a proposed sitemap, estimated timeline and project costs

2

Web Design

Create a website wireframe (blueprint) that will detail how the site will function, how it fits together and how the different website areas flow together.

3

Web Development

Develop the mobile ready website based on the wireframe. After many reviews and sign-off the next step is deploy it into production.

4

Maintenance

I'll be available to make follow on updates and enhancements that might needed to the website post deployment.
